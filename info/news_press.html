News & Press

March 22, 2015

I won The National Trumpet Competition - Jazz Division!!

This past weekend, I competed against eleven fantastic trumpet players. The first semi-final round took place on Friday afternoon and was followed by a final judging Saturday afternoon. The finals (three trumpet players left) were very exciting and nerve-wracking.

I'm very happy to say that my friend from Milwaukee, Wisconsin - Alec Aldred - and I came on top with the number 1 and 2 spots!
